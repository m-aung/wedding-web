# Key Design Decisions

1. Ceremony begins at 3:30 PM.
2. Cocktail hour lasts exactly 1 hour.
3. Reception begins at 5:00 PM.
4. First dance occurs near sunset for best photos.
5. Karaoke starts after dinner and dancing.
6. Wedding ends at approximately 10:00 PM.
7. Timeline optimized for:

   * Natural light photography
   * Small guest count
   * DIY sound system
   * Relaxed atmosphere
   * Minimal staffing requirements

---

# Sound System Notes

Ceremony Area:

* Dedicated speaker pair
* Wireless microphone for officiant and vows

Reception Area:

* Main speakers remain in place
* Mixer controls speeches, music, and karaoke

Music Flow:

1. Pre-ceremony music
2. Ceremony music
3. Cocktail hour playlist
4. Dinner background music
5. First dance song
6. Dancing playlist
7. Karaoke

No DJ required.
